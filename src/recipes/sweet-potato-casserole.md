ingredients:
- 3 cups hot, mashed, cooked sweet potatoes
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 large eggs
- 1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar
- 1/3 cup unsifted all-purpose flour
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 3 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened

---

1. In large bowl, combine sweet potatoes, sugar, salt, eggs, 1/4 cup butter, vanilla, and milk. Place in a greased 1 1/2 quart casserole or baking dish.

2. In small bowl, combine brown sugar, flour, pecans, and remaining butter. Top sweet potato casserole with crumbled mixture. Bake in a 350Â°F oven for 35 minutes. If chilled, bake for 45 minutes.
